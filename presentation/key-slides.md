## Presenter Tips

1. **Speak Clearly**: Slower than normal conversation
2. **Make Eye Contact**: Look at audience, not slides
3. **Demo Smooth**: Practice transitions
4. **Time Awareness**: Have visible clock
5. **Backup Plan**: Screenshots ready if tech fails
6. **Energy**: Enthusiasm is contagious
7. **Pause**: Let important points land
